# Privacy Policy
Thank you for using HypeCheck! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

<br>

## 1. Information We Collect
HypeCheck is designed to help you make smarter shopping decisions. We may collect the following types of information:

- Product Data: Product links and names you submit for analysis.
- Scan History: Your past hype checks, stored locally on your device.
- Usage Data: Anonymous data such as session length, app crashes, or feature usage, to improve performance and user experience.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless explicitly stated.

<br>

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information you provide to:

- Analyze products and generate Buy, Wait, or Skip verdicts.
- Provide proof scores, fair-price checks, and hype risk assessments.
- Improve app performance and user experience.

Your scan history is stored locally on your device.

<br>

## 3. Third-Party Services
HypeCheck may use trusted third-party tools for analytics or crash reporting. These services may collect limited technical data, such as device type or operating system version, but no personal data is shared.

We may update this section if new services are added in future versions.

<br>

## 4. Data Security
We take data security seriously. All data is stored securely on your device and any network requests are transmitted using industry-standard encryption.

You are responsible for protecting your device and any backups you make.

<br>

## 5. Your Rights
You have full control over your data. You can:

- View your scan history at any time.
- Delete your data at any time.
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data.

<br>

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of any major changes through the app or App Store listing.

<br>
